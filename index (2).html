Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the US?

Tacos are believed to have first appeared in the United States in the late 19th century.The earliest documented mention of tacos in the U.S. is from a newspaper article in 1905, referring to a "taqueria" in Los Angeles.They were popularized by Taco bell which made them known nationwide.
